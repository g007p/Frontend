Copper Coin is an independent multilingual publishing company. Founded in December 2013, the company is devoted to celebrating, nurturing and preserving the most interesting voices in Indian as well as world poetry, fiction and nonfiction in English, Hindi, Marathi and Punjabi. While our obsessions traverse multiple genres, our love remains precise in its passion for extraordinary poetry.
